Becoming a Member of the Belgian Bitcoin Association

How to become a member

We intend to automate the registration process later. For the time being, please send an email at info@bitcoinassociation.be. We'll get in touch with you right away.

Benefits

Members get the support of the association. There are specific benefits attached to the various types of membership.

Types of Membership

Full Members

Full members are expected to make an active contribution to support the association in any way that is within their means, e.g. by volunteering, organising events, etcetera. They have the right to determine the course of the association, to vote in the general assembly, to nominate directors, to be eligible as member of the board of directors.

One can become a full member after a period of 6 months after admission as adhering member.

Adhering Members

Adhering members have the right to participate in all events organised by the association, to participate in the general assembly as an observer, to receive promotional material, ...

Individual and Industry Memberships

Members can be either individuals or companies.
