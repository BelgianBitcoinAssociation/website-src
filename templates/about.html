About the Belgian Bitcoin Association

The Belgian Bitcoin association is organized under the form of an ASBL/VZW. It was founded on November 20th 2013 and its registration at the National Bank is on-going.

Membership to the association is open.

The association is run day-by-day by a Board of Directors. Directors have been elected by the members present at the foundation meeting, after a call for candidates.

Board of Directors

Arne Brutschy

German national Arne Brutschy is formally trained as computer scientist, he currently works as a researcher in artificial intelligence and swarm robotics at IRIDIA in the University of Brussels. As a Bitcoin evangelist and entrepreneur, he is well known in the Bitcoin scene and the co-organiser of the Brussels Bitcoin Meetup Group.

Jérémie Dubois-Lacoste

French born Jérémie is currently completing his PhD at IRIDIA, the artificial intelligence laboratory of the University of Brussels. He is known on the Bitcoin circuit as having a deep knowledge of the Bitcoin Protocol, has appeared on radio and television and is a co-founded the Brussels Bitcoin Meetup Group.

Chris D’Costa

Chris is a native Brit living and working in Belgium. For the last fifteen years he has worked as an independant tech consultant. He writes a bitcoin blog "With this in Mind". He has a good technical knowledge of Bitcoin and since been working on a private project to build a hardware bitcoin wallet called Meek.

Filip is a self-employed Belgian Entrepreneur working with various businesses including 3D printing, Bitcoin mining, Bitcoin payment processing and other projects. Filip is passionate about innovation and a hands-on type of person, featuring recently on Belgian radio. "Standing still isn’t an option". Involved in Bitcoin since block #227676.
