Support, education and promotion

Once you start using Bitcoins you quickly learn how easy and flexible it is. But we know that at the beginning everybody needs some help and support.

All of us in the Bitcoin community have gone through this learning phase so we are in a good position to help share the knowledge.

Bitcoin is designed to allow you to become your own bank, and because of this you will need to think about your money and it's security much more carefully.

That's why we provide information in the form of courses, workshops, and in-person support both practical and technical. We focus on all our members and their needs, beginners and experts alike.

Representing Bitcoin in Belgium and beyond

As a group we represent our members towards policy makers, the national bank, the press/media and any other interested party.

Bitcoin is like nothing the world has ever known before. Therefore it is normal that our members may have difficulty understanding how to apply relevant laws and regulations. Indeed even the law makers do not fully understand this new technology yet.

Because we represent our members and our directors are already experienced professionals, users and technologists, we are able to represent the technology fairly in all the major forums.

Providing clarity and understanding

The word about Bitcoin is spreading fast but often good information gets lost leading to misconceptions, especially in the media.

As a group of specialists, the Belgian Bitcoin Association works hard to clarify all aspects of Bitcoin, from the day-to-day transactions to the security and cryptography aspects.

Our interest is to support Bitcoin, and a duty to provide the best, most accurate information we can.
